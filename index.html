Who are we?

Established with 30 years of industry expertise, Srivarsha Food Products India Limited stands as a trusted leader in the manufacturing, supplying, and exporting of IQF Fruits & Vegetables, concentrates, and pulps, showcasing an extensive product line exceeding 22 offerings. With a dominant presence in the global IQF F & V market, we proudly represent Indian produce on the international stage. Alongside, we have cultivated partnerships with over 5000 farmers, aiding and empowering agricultural communities while ensuring a sustainable supply chain.
