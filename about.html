Company Overview

At Srivarsha Food Product India Limited, efficiency isn't just a goal—it's our commitment to your success.

Established with 30 years of industry expertise, Srivarsha Food Products India Limited stands as a trusted leader in the manufacturing, supplying, and exporting of IQF Fruits & Vegetables, concentrates, and pulps, showcasing an extensive product line exceeding 22 offerings. With a dominant presence in the global IQF F & V market, we proudly represent Indian produce on the international stage. Alongside, we have cultivated partnerships with over 5000 farmers, aiding and empowering agricultural communities while ensuring a sustainable supply chain.

Experienced promoters lead our company with approximately 40 years of industry experience. Our in-house manufacturing operating system enables real-time tracking, quality assurance, and seamless communication throughout the production process.

Our Mission

Our mission prioritizes health and nutrition. We use natural, aseptic thermal procedures to maintain purity in our fruits and vegetables, ensuring freshness and extended shelf life without any additives. Our IQF technology and cold chain management uphold product integrity



Our Vision

As a family-owned business rooted in farming heritage, our vision is to nurture and sustain Mother Earth while fostering ethical farming practices. We strive for continuous value addition, integrating at grassroots levels to uplift socio-economic conditions within our communities.



Our Objective

We emphasize ethical practices and food safety, adhering to global industry standards through continuous compliance with GMP and GAP. This commitment ensures that we consistently deliver value to our customers, staying aligned with global food industry trends.
