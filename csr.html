Corporate Social Responsibility

  • Home
  • Sri Varsha
Fertilizer Procurements

We assist farmers in obtaining quality fertilizers at affordable costs, saving them 25-30% from the maximum retail price. We ensure these fertilizers meet crop needs, promoting healthier growth and higher yields. Our efforts include guidance on optimal use and workshops on sustainable practices, contributing to long-term soil health and productivity.

Seeds Procurement

We help farmers acquire high-quality seeds to maximize yield. Working with agricultural experts, we select pest and disease-resistant seeds. Through bulk purchasing, we secure favorable prices. Additionally, we provide training on seed treatment and storage techniques to enhance germination rates and crop success.

Financing

Our financing solutions include personalized guidance to help farmers secure loans from trusted banks. We assist with loan applications, provide financial literacy programs, and help farmers manage their finances effectively. This support enables investment in advanced technologies and infrastructure, driving sustainable growth.

Insurances

We provide access to crop, life, and medical insurance, safeguarding farmers and their families against unforeseen challenges. Our partnerships with insurance providers offer tailored policies and support throughout the claims process. Educational initiatives raise awareness about insurance benefits, promoting long-term security.

Coordination with Government Authorities

We help farmers obtain subsidies and loan waivers by collaborating with government authorities. Our team assists with applications and advocates for policies supporting agricultural development. By bridging the gap between farmers and the government, we streamline processes and reduce bureaucratic hurdles

Continuous Research & Training

We invest in research and training to enhance agricultural practices and find innovative solutions. For instance, we are working on extracting mild powder from raw papaya to increase farmers' income. Regular workshops disseminate the latest findings, empowering farmers to adopt advanced technologies and methods.

Financial Protection

We provide comprehensive financial protection for farmers, mitigating risks related to illness, accidents, old age, and death. Our offerings include pension plans, disability coverage, and life insurance. Through partnerships, we ensure affordable financial protection, enabling farmers to focus on their work without financial worries.

Welfare Support

Our program offers social protection and welfare support to self-employed farmers, addressing healthcare, education, housing, and legal aid. We provide access to health clinics, scholarships, and housing assistance. By creating a robust social safety net, we help farmers and their families thrive amidst agricultural uncertainties.
