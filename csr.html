Corporate Social Responsibility

  • Home
  • Sri Varsha
Fertilizer Procurements

We assist farmers in obtaining suitable and quality fertilizers at affordable costs, resulting in savings of about 25-30% from the maximum retail price for over 500 farmers


Seeds Procurement

We provide support in acquiring the right quality of seeds to maximize yield


Financing

Our holistic financing solutions encompass personalized guidance and support, aiding farmers in securing loans from trusted banks and financial institutions. This ensures not only the cultivation of crops but also personal advancement and long-term financial stability..


Insurances

We streamline access to a range of insurance options, including crop, life, and medical insurance, safeguarding farmers and their families against unforeseen challenges and promoting long-term security and well-being.


Coordination with Government Authorities

We collaborate with government authorities to help farmers, especially in forestry and horticulture, obtain subsidies and farm loan waivers.


Continuous Research & Training

We invest in ongoing research and training initiatives aimed at enhancing agricultural practices and exploring innovative solutions. For example, we are working on extracting mild powder from raw papaya to double farmers' income from the same crop.


Financial Protection

We ensure comprehensive financial protection for farmers, mitigating risks associated with illness, work-related accidents, old age, and death. Our initiatives safeguard livelihoods, fostering resilience and peace of mind within farming communities.


Welfare Support

Our program focuses on providing self-employed farmers with the best possible social protection and offers welfare support to cushion the impact of structural changes in agriculture.
